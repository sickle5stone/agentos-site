Local-First, By Default

Nothing leaves your machine. Unless you ask.

Reading and analysis happen on your device. Two clearly-labeled actions are the only way data ever goes off-device, and only to a destination you configure.

Stays on your device

  • check_circle Your config is read locally
  • check_circle Embeddings run on-device (WebAssembly)
  • check_circle Secret-looking files (.env, keys) are refused by default
  • check_circle API keys encrypted in the OS keychain
